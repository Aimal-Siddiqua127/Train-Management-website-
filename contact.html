Frequently Asked Questions

How can I change or cancel my booking?

You can change or cancel your booking through the "My Tickets" section of your account. Changes and cancellations made within the first hour of booking are free of charge. After that, fees may apply based on our policy.

What is the baggage allowance on trains?

Each passenger is allowed to bring two pieces of luggage (up to 50 lbs each) and one personal item. Additional or oversized baggage may incur extra charges. Please check specific train information for any variations.

How early should I arrive at the station?

We recommend arriving at least 30 minutes before your train's scheduled departure time. This allows sufficient time for check-in, security procedures, and boarding.

Are there discounts available for children or seniors?

Yes, we offer discounted fares for children (ages 2-12) and seniors (65+). Children under 2 travel free when sitting on an adult's lap. Military personnel and students may also be eligible for discounts with valid ID.
