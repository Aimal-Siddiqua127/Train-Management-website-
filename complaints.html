Frequently Asked Questions

How long will it take to resolve my complaint?

We aim to resolve all complaints within 14 days. Complex cases may take longer, but we'll keep you updated throughout the process.

What compensation can I expect for a delayed train?

Compensation depends on the length of the delay. For delays of 30-60 minutes, you may receive 25% of your fare. For delays over 60 minutes, you may receive 50% of your fare. Delays over 2 hours may qualify for a full refund.

Can I file a complaint on behalf of someone else?

Yes, you can file a complaint on behalf of someone else, but we'll need written authorization from that person unless you're their legal guardian or have power of attorney.

What if I'm not satisfied with the resolution?

If you're not satisfied with our resolution, you can request an escalation to our Customer Relations Manager. If you're still not satisfied after this step, you can contact the relevant transport regulatory authority.
